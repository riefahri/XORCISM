NIST SP 800-30 — Risk Assessment

Conduct cyber risk assessments using NIST SP 800-30 Rev.1 (Guide for Conducting Risk Assessments) — the US federal counterpart of EBIOS RM. Each assessment identifies threat sources (adversarial & non-adversarial), the threat events they can initiate, the vulnerabilities and predisposing conditions they exploit, and determines risk as overall likelihood × level of impact on the 800-30 semi-quantitative scale (Very Low → Very High).
