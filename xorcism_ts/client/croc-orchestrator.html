Agentic CROC orchestrator

The autonomous layer over the CROC loop. It watches the LOOPEVENT bus for new high/critical signals, picks the right analyst copilot, and drafts a verdict + recommended action — queued here for your approval. Nothing side-effectful runs without a human: the orchestrator triages at machine speed, you stay in the loop. Approve records the decision (and notifies); Dismiss closes it.
