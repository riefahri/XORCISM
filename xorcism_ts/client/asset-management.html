Asset Management

One governance view over your asset estate — ownership, exposure, backup posture, control & BIA coverage, and open vulnerability load (KEV / critical). Each asset is checked for the hygiene gaps that matter: crown jewels exposed to the Internet, critical assets with no backup, owner-less assets, KEV/critical vulnerabilities, missing controls or BIA, PII at risk, and stale / end-of-life assets. Import assets from ARF (SCAP)

Upload a NIST Asset Reporting Format 1.1 (NISTIR 7694) / Asset Identification XML document — e.g. an OpenSCAP result file or a XORCISM ARF export. Each computing-device becomes an asset (hostname, FQDN, IPv4/IPv6, CPE); a XORCISM extension block restores criticality, environment and value when present.
