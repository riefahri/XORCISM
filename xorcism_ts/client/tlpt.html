TLPT — Threat-Led Penetration Testing (TIBER-EU)

Run controlled, intelligence-led red-team tests against your live critical systems following the ECB TIBER-EU framework — the methodology DORA references for advanced (TLPT) testing of financial entities. Track the three phases (Preparation → Testing → Closure) with their canonical milestones & sign-offs, the intelligence-led attack scenarios, the flags (critical-function targets) and whether each was reached / detected / prevented, the red-team findings & remediation, and the team roster (White / Control / Blue / Red / TI / authority). The scorecard measures resilience (Prevent · Detect · Respond), not just whether the testers got in.
