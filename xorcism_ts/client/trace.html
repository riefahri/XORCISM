TRACE — structured, evidence-driven threat modeling

TRACE (Oak Security) turns heterogeneous sources into a structured model of five first-class objects — Threat actors, Roles, Assets, Critical invariants and Edges — then runs STRIDE, builds attack trees, inspects collusion/coordination surfaces, and produces a mitigation roadmap, through a deliberately sequential 6-phase workflow with human approval gates. Every model object records its source as evidence (or is flagged an inferred assumption), and every threat should trace back to an object.
