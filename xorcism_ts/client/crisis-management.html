Crisis Management & Tabletop Exercises

Run and track tabletop exercises (TTX) against a library of crisis scenarios (ransomware, data breach, DDoS, insider, supply-chain…). Each exercise is an audit of type Tabletop Exercise — so its observations become improvement actions (findings) and its after-action report a linked document. The worklist surfaces what matters: overdue improvement actions, scenarios never exercised, and exercises with no after-action report. A single crisis-readiness score blends exercise completion with scenario coverage. Launch an exercise from any scenario below; manage entries under Exercises / Injects / Improvement actions.
