Identity Threat Detection & Response (ITDR)

Detects identity-based attacks from your sign-in telemetry (IDENTITYSIGNIN) and identity posture (IDENTITY) — brute force, password spraying, impossible travel, MFA fatigue, IdP-flagged risk, credential compromise, dormant-account reactivation, and privileged exposure. Each detection is mapped to a MITRE ATT&CK technique with a recommended response; triage detections and raise an incident when needed.
