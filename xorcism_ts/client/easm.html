External Attack Surface Management

The attacker's outside-in view of your estate: every internet-facing asset, why it is exposed (public IP, website, inbound traffic, a monitored endpoint or a public-facing flag), the services & ports it presents, its TLS/certificate posture and the externally-reachable vulnerabilities it carries (KEV / critical) — ranked by an exposure score. Shadow exposure (internet-reachable but never declared public-facing) and surface drift (newly exposed assets) are flagged for action. Snapshot the surface to measure drift over time.
