Govern the security of your operational technology — ICS / SCADA / DCS / PLC / IoT — against IEC 62443 and NIST SP 800-82. OT assets are the ones tagged ot / ics / scada / iot…; IEC 62443 zones & conduits carry target/achieved Security Levels (SL 1-4), and the seeded requirement catalogues (62443-3-3 FR1-7 / NIST 800-82) back the assessments.
