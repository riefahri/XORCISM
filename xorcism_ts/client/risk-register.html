Risk Register

One governance view over the risk register — every risk's inherent → current → residual level, its treatment (strategy, plan, owner, review) and its CRQ/FAIR quantification (Annualized Loss Expectancy). Each risk carries a 0-100 priority score; the worklist surfaces the gaps that matter: high/critical residual risks left untreated, risks accepted without justification, overdue reviews, treatments past their target date, and unowned risks. Manage entries under Risk entries; decompose a loss with FAIR-MAM.
