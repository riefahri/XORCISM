SPRS — NIST SP 800-171 self-assessment score

The DoD Supplier Performance Risk System (SPRS) score for the 110 NIST SP 800-171 Rev 2 requirements: start at 110 and subtract each not-met requirement's weight (1 / 3 / 5); a POA&M item still counts as not-met until closed; partial implementation earns partial credit; N/A is excluded. Required for DoD contractors (DFARS 252.204-7012/-7019/-7020) and the basis of CMMC Level 2. Weights follow the DoD Assessment Methodology and are editable per requirement — validate against your authoritative copy.
