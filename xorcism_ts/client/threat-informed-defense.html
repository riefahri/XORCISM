Threat-Informed Defense

For every MITRE ATT&CK technique, XORCISM scores how much adversaries use it (ecosystem prevalence from ATT&CK groups, boosted by your local CTI & hunts) against whether you detect it (Sigma rules), mitigate it (D3FEND + ATT&CK mitigations) and test it (Atomic Red Team). The result is a single threat-weighted program score and a prioritised gap worklist — the highest-threat techniques with the weakest defence. Close gaps from Purple team (detection), D3FEND (mitigation) and the BAS / Atomic tests (validation).
