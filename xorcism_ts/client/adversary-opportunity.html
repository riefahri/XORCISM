Adversary Opportunity Index — the path-organized "threat debt" top-line

One derived number for the true adversary opportunity in your environment: every gap that sits on a viable attack path to a crown jewel (the Attack Path Test), weighted by exploitability, by the adversaries that actually use those techniques, by business impact, and net of the controls you can prove. The attacker's-eye sibling of the Enterprise Risk Score. Lower is better; it moves only when real exposure changes.
