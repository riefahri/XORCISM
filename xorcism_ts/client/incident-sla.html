Incidents vs asset SLAs & BIA RTOs

Each incident's Duration (hours to resolve) is measured against two recovery targets on every affected asset: the resolution SLA (ASSET.SLAResolutionHours) and the Recovery Time Objective from its BIA entry (BIAENTRY.RTO). Breaches of either surface here.
